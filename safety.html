Safety & Ethics

Artist safety, consent, and autonomy are non-negotiable principles at Étoile Creative Lounge.

  • No coercion or pressure of any kind
  • No sexual, romantic, or personal obligations
  • Artists maintain personal boundaries
  • Privacy and identity protection respected
  • Clear separation between professional and personal roles

Any future live events, collaborations, or physical venues will operate under documented safety procedures.
